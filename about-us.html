About Us

Museo De San Pedro is located at #122 Magsasaysay, San Pedro City, Laguna. It's only a 17-minute drive from HBC San Pedro. You can also take a jeepney ride on the "estrella trip" route, heading to Magsasay (beside HBC). Advise the driver to drop you off at "Museo De San Pedro" in front of Villa Consolascion. Alternatively, use the Waze Mobile App to locate us.
